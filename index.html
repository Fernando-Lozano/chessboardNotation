Master The Chessboard

Memorize the notation of the chessboard in a fun way. Instead of focusing on learning each individual square, which is 64 squares, focus on learning the rows and columns, which is only 16 things to memorize. First, memorize the columns from every angle. Then, memorize the rows from every angle. Finally, memorize the squares from every angle.
